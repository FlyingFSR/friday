# Code of Conduct

Friday is a small open-source project. The goal is to make it easy for people to
test, report problems, and contribute without friction.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but stay accountable for your words and actions.
- Keep issue and pull request discussion focused on the project.
- Share reproduction details, test results, and trade-offs when possible.
- Respect privacy: do not post someone else's audio, transcripts, credentials,
  private paths, or personal information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent.
- Repeated off-topic comments that make project maintenance harder.
- Intentionally misleading bug reports, security reports, or attribution claims.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may edit, hide, or delete comments; close issues; block accounts;
or otherwise limit participation when behavior harms the project or other
participants.

If you see a problem, open an issue with the minimum context needed, or contact
the maintainer through GitHub.
